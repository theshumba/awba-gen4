8Zero-CDN assertion

Every face on this page is self-hosted and same-origin — a PWA that promises offline can't depend on a third-party font host.

Confirm it yourself

Open DevTools › Network, filter by font, reload: every request must be same-origin shared/fonts/… — zero Google-Fonts-CDN requests. If you see any third-party font host, the zero-CDN guarantee is broken.
